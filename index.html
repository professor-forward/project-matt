ABOUT US

Hometown Histories is a passion project originally designed by Matt Tippin (#300066841) for CSI3140, Winter 2022. The intent of this project is to provide uOttawa students a safe and interactive way to share the varied histories of the towns they grew up in with other uOttawa students.
